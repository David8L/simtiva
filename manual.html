Welcome to SimTIVA Manual

This is the user manual for simtiva.app, a web app to simulate target-controlled infusion (TCI).

Contents

Getting Started

On the first screen, you may enter patient characteristics and choose a model. According to the model, there may be different patient parameters that are required. On the top, you can choose between "Adult" and "Paediatric" modes.
